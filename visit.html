Transportation Options

By Car

Free parking available in our main lot. Enter from Botanical Lane. EV charging stations available.

Public Transit

Take Metro Bus #12 or #24 to the "Garden Gate" stop, just steps from our entrance.

Bicycle

Bike racks available at the main entrance. The Greenway Trail connects directly to the garden.

Walking

Located just 15 minutes from downtown via the Riverwalk path.

your experience

What to Expect

Everything you need to know before you arrive.

Trails & Terrain

Over 7 miles of trails ranging from easy boardwalks to moderate hills. Wear comfortable walking shoes and dress for the weather.

Suggested Visit Time

Most visitors spend 2-3 hours exploring. Plan extra time for the visitor center, gift shop, and photography stops.

Pets

Well-behaved dogs on leashes are welcome on most trails. Service animals always welcome. Pet waste stations available.

Photography

Personal photography welcome. For professional or commercial photography, please contact us in advance.

Weather

The garden is open rain or shine. In case of severe weather, check our website or social media for updates.

Accessibility

Most main paths are wheelchair and stroller accessible. Manual wheelchairs available at the visitor center.

everyone welcome

Accessibility

We're committed to making the garden accessible to all visitors.

Wheelchair Access

All major paths and facilities are wheelchair accessible. Manual wheelchairs available free at the visitor center (first-come, first-served).

Hearing Assistance

Printed guides available. Some programs offer ASL interpretation with 2 weeks advance notice.

Visual Impairment

Braille guides available. Many trails have tactile elements and sensory experiences.

Accessible Facilities

Accessible restrooms at visitor center and main pavilion. Family restrooms available.
