Morning Bird Walk

Date & Time

Every Saturday, 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Location

Candler Woods Trailhead

Capacity

15 participants

About This Event

Start your weekend with the soothing sounds of nature. Our Morning Bird Walk is a gentle, guided exploration through Candler Woods, one of the garden's most diverse habitats. Led by experienced naturalist Dr. Sarah Chen, this walk is perfect for both beginners and experienced birders.

We'll explore old-growth forest trails, listening for calls and spotting migratory and resident species. Binoculars are provided, but feel free to bring your own. The walk covers approximately 1.5 miles of easy terrain with frequent stops for observation and discussion.

What You'll Experience

  • Identify 15-20 bird species including warblers, woodpeckers, and raptors
  • Learn to recognize birds by their calls and songs
  • Understand bird behavior and habitat preferences
  • Photography tips for capturing birds in the wild
  • Complimentary field guide and checklist

What to Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Water bottle
  • Camera (optional)
  • Personal binoculars (optional)

Meeting Point

Meet at the Candler Woods Trailhead, located near the main parking lot. Look for the wooden archway and our naturalist holding binoculars. Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to check in.

Ticket Information

  • Adult (18-64) $15
  • Senior (65+) $12
  • Student (with ID) $10
  • Child (under 12) $8
  • Dashboard Free
