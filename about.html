Cultivating connections with nature

Since 1987, we've been a sanctuary for native ecosystems and a place for people to find peace, wonder, and knowledge in the heart of the city.

our purpose

Preserve, Educate, Inspire

Groww Garden is more than a collection of plants – we're a living laboratory and a refuge for both wildlife and weary souls. Our mission is to protect biodiversity, offer environmental education, and foster a deep appreciation for the natural world.

We believe that when people connect with nature, they become its stewards. Every gallery, every program, and every conserved acre reflects this commitment.

roots & growth

Our story

From a small community garden to a renowned botanical preserve.

1987

Founding vision

A group of local naturalists and volunteers transform a neglected city lot into the first community garden, planting 50 native trees.

1995

Wetland restoration

Heron Wetland is restored, bringing back native aquatic plants and attracting great blue herons for the first time in decades.

2004

Education center opens

The Groww Center opens, offering year-round classes, school field trips, and a native plant nursery.

2023

80 acres preserved

With community support, we expand to 80 contiguous acres, protecting critical habitat for over 200 species.
