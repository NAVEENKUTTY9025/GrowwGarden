welcome to groww garden

Your urban sanctuary

Nestled in the heart of the city, our 80-acre preserve offers a peaceful escape into native woodlands, wetlands, and prairie. Open every day.

restoration in action

Bringing back the woodland understory

Thanks to 150 volunteers, we've planted over 1,200 native wildflowers and ferns in Candler Woods this spring. The trillium and bloodroot are already attracting early pollinators. See how you can help with our next planting day.
