Frequently asked

Quick answers to common inquiries

Are Gallery stroller-friendly?
Yes! Fern Canyon gallery and Prairie Loop are packed gravel and boardwalk, suitable for strollers and wheelchairs.
Can i bring my own food?
Absolutely. Picnic tables are available near the visitor center. Please pack out all trash.
Is there wifi on site?
Free wifi is available at the visitor center and café area. Gallery are tech-free zones.
Do you offer group tours?
Yes, guided group tours available for 10+ people. Please book at least 2 weeks in advance.
